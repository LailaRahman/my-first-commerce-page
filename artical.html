Need For Speed: Rivals PC – 2015 Ford Mustang GT

Apr 11, 2020 4,320 Technology

It literally has the highest top speed among T1, even higher than the 2014 Ford Mustang GT, in fact even higher than most T2 and T3 cars! On top of that, it has much better acceleration.

Need For Speed: Rivals PC 720p HD Fully Upgraded 2015 Ford Mustang GT gameplay with keyboard. The 2015 Ford Mustang GT is a new and free DLC car from the recent NFS Rivals update. This video showcases the Ford Mustang GT’s performance by completing Chapter 4 Apex Predators speed list after fully upgraded in 1 time trial and 1 race event and 1 hot pursuit events.   • Top Speed: 6.5 – 8.5
  • Acceleration: 5.8 – 7.1

  • Strength: 6.4 – 7.4
  • Durability: 5.8 – 8.3

Review:

This is the latest Ford Mustang and it’s been so long after we have so many Shelby powered variants. So it’s a shock for EA to give us a free car. And they gave a really good… T1 car… that should be T2. In other words, this is supposed to be a T2 car based on it’s performance and it’s stats.
